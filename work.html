FairGame Initiative

FairGame is a nonprofit I built from scratch to close the resource and opportunity gap in STEM education — specifically the science fair pipeline that dramatically shaped my own path.

Science fairs at the national level are disproportionately won by students who have access to lab space, mentors with credentials, and institutional backing. FairGame exists to change that by working directly with school-level science fairs and connecting students to the resources, guidance, and advocacy they need to compete on an equal footing.

  • Manages a 5-person team across outreach, operations, and programming
  • Oversees nonprofit finances, legal compliance, and organizational development
  • Directed outreach to 10+ school-level science fairs
  • Bridges the resource and mentorship gap for underserved student researchers
